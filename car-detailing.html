Comprehensive detailing and reconditioning services for both personal and fleet vehicles.


Specializing in both interior and exterior detailing for fleet or personal cars, C & C Power Washing, Detail, & Polishing Inc tailors detail packages to meet the unique needs of each customer.

Independent drivers trust our services to maintain the impeccable appearance of their trucks, while businesses rely on us to safeguard their brand image. Additionally, we offer fleet management services upon request, facilitating comprehensive full and semi-fleet detailing.

Our mission is to deliver a thorough and impeccable cleaning for every vehicle that comes to us. When you arrive at our shop to collect your car, we guarantee it will be shining. Expect us to:

  • Restore your vehicle's shine with our expert services: Trust our experienced technicians to deliver a flawless clear coat finish, ensuring a pristine and polished appearance.
  • Vehicle Protection: We'll eliminate all dust and dirt from your vehicle, and for added protection, we can apply a coating to safeguard the paint.
  • Eliminate Vehicle Paint Oxidation: Combat paint oxidation caused by UV rays with our restoration services for vehicles and equipment. We'll improve the appearance and extend the lifespan by eliminating oxidation. Contact us to address paint swirls and scratches on your vehicle.
